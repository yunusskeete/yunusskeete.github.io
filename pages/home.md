I'm an applied AI engineer with a background in research science and software engineering. I'm currently Founder & CEO of [Spatial Intelligence](https://www.spatial-intelligence.co.uk/), a deeptech AI startup building tools for machines that understand the structure and content of physical environments.

My work spans the gap between fundamental research and production systems - from designing self-supervised world models and reasoning architectures, to shipping scalable AI-powered products on cloud infrastructure.

I care about outcomes over output and impact over influence. I enjoy providing (and receiving!) mentorship and thrive in tightly-knit, high-agency interdisciplinary teams. I think the best researchers and engineers are creatives who are genuinely fascinated by the technology they build, and humble enough to keep learning from everyone around them.

# Work

## [Research]({% link pages/research.md %})

My research centres on **spatial intelligence** - specifically, how machines can learn structured, geometrically grounded representations of the world without explicit supervision. Active areas include self-supervised representation learning (JEPAs), object-centric world modelling, and hierarchical latent-space reasoning (Latent Cycle Transformers). Applied work includes spatially-grounded AI agents and 3D scene generation from text descriptions.

## [Engineering]({% link pages/engineering.md %})

I build across the full stack - Python APIs, cloud infrastructure (Azure, AWS), ML systems, and TypeScript frontends - with a focus on reliable, scalable AI products. Current engineering work includes an async, GPU-bound 3D scene generation pipeline using LLM orchestration, vector search, and event-driven autoscaling on Azure Container Apps.
